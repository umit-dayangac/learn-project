Spaghetti Carbonara

A classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. It's simple yet incredibly flavorful, perfect for a quick weeknight dinner.

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Salt & pepper

Instructions

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. In a pan, cook the pancetta until crispy.
  3. Beat the eggs and mix with cheese, salt, and pepper.
  4. Combine everything in the pan and serve immediately.
